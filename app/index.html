Island Accommodations.For all you mainlanders!

Okay, so here is the scoop... We live in Honolulu/Town (on the South Shore), which is very close to the Waikiki tourist area. This is where all the restaurants and hotels are located. Our wedding will take place in Haliewa/Kahuku, which is on the North Shore. The North Shore is about a 45 minute drive from South Shore. We recommend staying in Waikiki or somewhere on the South Shore prior to the wedding and then on the North Shore from the day before to the day after the wedding. This way we can meet up and plan activities/beach days together and you won't have to drive all the way back to town after the wedding. Depending on how many people are in your party will help determine the most cost effect accommodations.

Hotel Only
