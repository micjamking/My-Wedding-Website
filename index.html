Island Accommodations.For all you mainlanders!

Okay, so here is the scoop... We live in Honolulu/Town (on the South Shore), which is very close to the Waikiki tourist area. This is where all the restaurants and hotels are located. Our wedding will take place in Haliewa/Kahuku, which is on the North Shore. The North Shore is about a 45 minute drive from South Shore. We recommend staying in Waikiki or somewhere on the South Shore prior to the wedding and then on the North Shore from the day before to the day after the wedding. This way we can meet up and plan activities/beach days together and you won't have to drive all the way back to town after the wedding. Depending on how many people are in your party, this will help determine the most cost effective accommodations.

Hotel Only

Island FunBeach, BBQ, & Cruising the Isle!

We want to enjoy your company and show you some of our favorite spots! Here is where we will be each day for an adventurous wedding week!

Friday & Saturday

  • June 6, 2014 & June 7, 2014

We hope to be welcoming you at the airport! Please let us know when you arrive so we can greet you Hawaiian Style! If you plan to arrive earlier than these days, give us a call and lets meet up!

Sunday

  • June 8, 2014

Get rid of your jet lag and meet us at Ala Moana Beach Park for a relaxing Sunday BBQ. Grab your drink of choice and bring something to throw on the grill! This is a great beach to break in your sea legs as the water is very calm. This beach is located on the south shore directly across from Ala Moana Shopping Center, the world's largest outdoor mall. Get some sun and enjoy some shopping!

Monday

  • June 9, 2014

Today we will be exploring the east side of the island and hosting a beach BBQ at Makapu'u Beach. The drive to this beach alone will confirm that this trip was worth the flight!

Monday night we hope you can join us for a Luau at Germain's Luau. Eat some traditional Hawaiian food, enjoy Hula dancing and learn about polynesian cultures. Please let us know if you plan to attend so we can get a group rate!

Tuesday

  • June 10, 2014

Another relaxing day at Lanikai's, a picturesque Hawaiian beach. No waves here, just a relaxing day with great food and company. Try your hand at stand up paddleboarding or kayak to some deserted islands in the distance. This is a local spot you don't want to miss!

Wednesday

  • June 11, 2014

Anyone up for a morning waterfall hike? Or how about a hike up Diamond Head Volcano? Let us know if you want to join, otherwise today will be all about resting up for the big day tomorrow!

Thursday

  • June 12, 2014

Wedding day on North Shore!

Come gather at Loulu Palms Estate for a celebration of life & love.

Friday

  • June 13, 2014

Recovering from the wedding - today is a great day to explore Turtle Bay Resort or Sunset beach, a close famous North Shore beach. Today is sure to be a great day as we celebrate our first day together as a married couple!

Saturday

  • June 14, 2014

Today we will be back at it again at Waimea Beach Park. You can enjoy some calm waves or if you are feeling adventurous, jump off the rock cliffs into the ocean. Not feeling up the beach? Check out the Waimea Botanical Garden just across the street. This is a beautiful garden is filled with Hawaiian flora and fauna which leads to the back of the valley and ends at a waterfall.

Sunday

  • June 15, 2014

Want to get away from the crowds? Let's head to Mokuleia on Oahu's northernmost tip. This secluded beach will be a great place to wrap up a week of adventure! There is nothing to eat on this side, so bring something to throw on the grill and plan to stay and relax for a while!
